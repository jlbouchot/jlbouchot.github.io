I am currently an assistant professor in the department of Mathematics and Statistics at Beijing Institute of Technology

As a researcher, I am generally interested in applications of sparse approximation and signal processing to general mathematical and engineering problems. I have contributed in very diverse areas including (in non logical order) Optimal Coherence Tomography; Numerical solutions to parametric PDEs; Numerical recovery of sparse signals; Metagenomics; ...

In Summer 2020 I will be part of the teaching staff at the Gene Golub SIAM Summer School on the Theory and Practice of Deep Learning. I will also be a guest lecturer at the Southeast Asian Mathematical Society School on Modern Trends in Signal and Data Processing.
In Summer 2019, I was teaching at the Academia Sinica in Taipei, as part of the Summer program on Mathematical Signal Processing and Data Analysis.

I am a fervent believer in Open science and reproducible research. As such most of my work and all of my recent works can be found online on various platforms: my research page; my list of publications; my software on GitHub; some recent papers on arXiv. You may also drop me an email, should you need more references.
